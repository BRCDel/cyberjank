Welcome to Alicante

Alicante is a megalopolis thriving in the middle of a wasteland left by nuclear war of ages past.
While the gritty streets seem inhospitable, they're a far better place to live in than the regions beyond the city.
Still, that doesn't mean it's safe. Crime is frequent, average quality of life remains low, and the corporations aren't too kind either.
Good luck out there. You'll probably need it.

Okay, but how do I actually play the game?

You'll be presented with three options at all times. Selecting one is as simple as clicking on it.
You can see your current status on the bottom of the screen. If your Health reaches zero, you die. Obviously.
Your Attack determines how hard you hit, your Defence determines how much damage you resist.
Critical hits are random for now. Chances should be around 10-15%.
