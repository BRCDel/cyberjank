Welcome to Alicante

Alicante is a megalopolis thriving in the middle of a wasteland left by nuclear war of ages past.
While the gritty streets seem inhospitable, they're a far better place to live in than the regions beyond the city.
Still, that doesn't mean it's safe. Crime is frequent, average quality of life remains low, and the corporations aren't too kind either.
Good luck out there. You'll probably need it.

Okay, but how do I actually play the game?

You'll be presented with up to three options at all times. Selecting one is as simple as clicking on it.
You can see your current status on the bottom of the screen. If your Health reaches zero, you die. Obviously.
Your Attack determines how hard you hit, your Defence determines how much damage you resist.
Critical hits are random for now. Chances should be around 10-15%.

What data does this game collect?

None that I know of. However, the game creates a save file with your current progress and stores it locally on your browser using the WebStorage API.
You can delete this file at any time with the Clear Data button on the bottom of the in-game UI.
The only cookies used are considered Strictly Functional - If the browser/page doesn't create it by default, it's not there.
However, full disclosure: This game is hosted with Github Pages. While Github Pages does not collect user data or store cookies, this is still worth mentioning for transparency.
To the best of my knowledge, cookies are not used. None of this site's code includes any use of cookies.
